FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this against Google's review policy?

No. The AI suggests options — the customer chooses, edits, and posts it themselves from their own Google account. We don't fake reviews and we don't gate ratings (low ratings can post too). This is the same pattern Birdeye, Podium, and other major platforms use.

Can the AI auto-post reviews to Google?

No platform can — Google has no public API for posting reviews, by design. What we do: copy the review to the customer's clipboard and one-tap open Google's review screen. They paste + submit. It's the maximum automation possible, by Google's rules.

What if a customer gives a bad rating?

You see it in your dashboard with the customer's tags (food, service, etc.) so you know exactly what went wrong. The customer can still post to Google if they want — but every honest review helps you improve faster.

How long does setup take?

Less than 5 minutes. You add your store name, category, logo, Google review URL, and SEO keywords — we generate the QR code instantly. Print it, place it, done.

Is customer data secure?

We don't collect any personal customer info — no name, email, or phone. Just the rating + tags they tap. Reviews are stored on encrypted Postgres servers (Supabase). Your owner login is bcrypt-hashed.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — month-to-month. Cancel from your dashboard, no questions asked. Your reviews stay on Google forever.
